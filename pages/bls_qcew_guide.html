BLS QCEW Data for Technical and Trade Schools

The U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics Quarterly Census of Employment and Wages (QCEW) provides comprehensive data covering employment, wages, and establishment counts by state and industry. This data is invaluable for analyzing workforce trends and economic patterns across different sectors, including technical and trade schools classified under NAICS 611500.

Step-by-Step Download Instructions

  1. Visit the BLS QCEW downloadable data files page
    Navigate to https://www.bls.gov/cew/downloadable-data-files.htm
  2. Focus on NAICS-Based Data Files section
    Look for the section containing NAICS-based data files, which provides data from 1990 onward with detailed industry classifications.
  3. Download the relevant year and quarter files
    Select and download the full industry CSV data files for the specific year and quarter you need for your analysis.
  4. Filter by NAICS code 611500
    Use Excel, Python, R, or other data analysis tools to filter the downloaded CSV files by NAICS code 611500 to isolate Technical and Trade Schools data.
  5. Alternative: Use BLS Data Slices
    Optionally, utilize BLS's industry-specific Data Slices feature for direct access to NAICS code-specific CSV files, which can save time on data filtering.
This comprehensive dataset enables researchers, policymakers, and educators to conduct in-depth analysis of employment trends, wage patterns, and industry growth in technical and trade schools across the United States.
Data source: U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics - QCEW
